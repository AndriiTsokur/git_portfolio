The company "Technion Co." is an automobile group which have their head quarters in Odessa. The dealers network covers the main regions in Ukraine: Kiev, Donetsk, Dnepropetrovsk, Krivoy Rog, Odessa and Nikolaev.

"Technion Co." - is one of the largest importers of automobiles, spare parts, accumulators and accessories.

The main direction of activity of "Technion Co." is the trade in automobiles produced by South Korean firms as "Hyundai", "Asia Motors" and "Kia" with guarantee and service. The firm offers all spectrum of models from small-displacement to sport automobiles and jeeps.

For 1997 "Technion Co." has been developing a new direction of import and trade of the best samples of the world auto market as "Mitsubishi", "Nissan", "Audi", "Honda", including automobiles of special purpose (ambulance and armoured of the cars for collections and operative transport). The system of their warranty is adjusted service.

The established customers of firm "Technion Co." are the largest banks of Ukraine including the National Ukrainian Bank, "Aval", Prominvestbank of Ukraine, and also the State Custom Service, The Ministry of Interior Affaires of Ukraine and many large commercial companies.

"Technion Co." represents in Ukraine interests of the largest manufacturers of accessories and accumulators, such as "Juma Al Majid Est" (UAA) and "Hyundai Corporation" (Southern Korea). All production delivered in Ukraine, is certified and marked by the highest quality.

The company has plans of development for 1998 of dealer networks and opening of new sales markets.
